> We build the free, open-source alternatives to the small, beautiful Mac apps that shouldn't cost $10 — written natively in Swift, running entirely on your machine. No Electron, no cloud, no telemetry. Licensed to protect user agency and education, and to keep large corporations from extracting profit from them.
